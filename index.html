About

Advanced Battle is a game that models itself after the old classic Advance Wars. For many of us that means were ready to jump on in! For others that maybe don't have that experience, here is a basic summary of the game:

You control an army, and each player takes turns moving their pieces. In a single turn, one player will move all his or her pieces and then the other player will move all their pieces. After moving a piece, you can have your troops engage in battle. The superiority of units is pretty intuitive: tanks will beat out soldiers and bombers will take out tanks.

The game revolves around controling certain cities. These cities add to how much money you earn per round, and you win the game by controling the other team's headquarters. You take over cities by liberating them with your infantry units.

Many things affect how your troops can fight: some spaces give you better cover than others, units have natural advantages over others, and make sure not to lose too much on any one front! There is a lot to learn about the game, and the best way to start is by jumping into a game!
