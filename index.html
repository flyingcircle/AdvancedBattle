Remember that one game?

As a child, you didn't have very many options as a kid. You basically played whatever your mom got you, and depending on your mother, that could be pretty lame.

But remember that one time she didn't screw up? Yeah, she saw that kid riding on top of a tank and thought, "Well that's cute!" And it ended up being one of the most rockin games you ever played.

Well guess what? Advanced Battle is a project that aims to bring back that old nostalgia of those hours wasted on your old Game Boy Advance. Relive that experience and this time, experience it with your friends!

Challenge your friends!

  • Select from a growing and wide array of maps!
  • Grow and build your army to overtake your opponent.
  • Play resembling, yet non-suable look-alikes of your favorite commanders!
